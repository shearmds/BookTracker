What it does

A simple, private library that travels with you.

🔎

Find any book

Search by title or author and add any book to your library in a tap — complete with cover art, author, and description.

📚

Track your shelf

Mark each book as Want to Read, In Progress, or Read. Add a star rating and your own private notes as you go.

☁️

Synced everywhere

Your library stays in sync between your Mac and iPhone. Link a device with a simple sync code — no account, no sign-up.
